# How to Create a Free Online Vision Board (No Signup Required)

Create a free digital vision board online with goal tracking, journaling, and Kanban view. No signup, no upload — 100% browser-based and private.

**Read time:** 9 min | **Category:** productivity

---

Open the FindUtils [Vision Board](/en/tools/vision-board), click "Add Vision," and start building your board with text notes, goals, drawings, images, and affirmations. No account, no signup, no email required — your board is saved automatically in your browser's local storage. The entire tool runs client-side, meaning nothing you add is ever uploaded to a server.

This guide walks you through every feature: creating cards, tracking goals with milestones, organizing work in Kanban view, journaling your mood, using focus mode, and backing up your data with JSON export. By the end, you'll have a fully functional digital vision board that helps you visualize and track your goals every day.
